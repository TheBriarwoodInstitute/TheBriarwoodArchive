Liam's Report - The Rift

Liam O'Connor

Name: Liam O'Connor
Age: 26
Nationality: Irish
Position(s): Technologist and Security Advisor
Date of Employment: 23/06/2052
Blood Type: A-
Known Family: Seamus O'Connor (Father, alive), Orla O'Connor (Mother, alive)

Liam O'Connor first encountered The Briarwood Institute while Ianto Cadigan and James Dalton were investigating frequent occurrences of a background process appearing on people's computers called HYDE.exe for short amounts of time. At the time of the occurrences, Liam was working in an IT department and volunteered to help investigate when Ianto and James showed up at his company's building to investigate as a computer in the building was the last known device to have had HYDE.exe appear. Ianto and James were impressed by Liam and eventually approached him to offer him a job.
