James Dalton

Name: James Dalton
Age: 26
Nationality: English
Position(s): Investigator and Combat Advisor
Date of Employment: 18/02/2052
Blood Type: B+
Known Family: Robert Dalton (Father, alive), Sophia Dalton (Sister, alive)

James Dalton first encountered The Briarwood Institute on the 17th of February 2052 while tracking down a creature of unknown origin, during which he encountered Ianto Cadigan. After this encounter, Ianto searched for information on James and approached him the following day and successfully recruited him to the institute, instantly becoming second in command as he was the only other employee at the time.
